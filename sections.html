The Magic of Music

Music is truly and utterly one of mankinds greatest creations. An artform which can: portray emotion, take you to a certain memory, portray a specific feeling and help you forget the common troubles of life. This is all achieved through sound waves and vibrations, making it truly incredible.

The Future of Music

There will always be a future for music and it's industry, but what that is, I truly do not know. My hope is I enjoy the next generation of music. I honestly believe music will never die, just evolve to newer and more advanced forms. Acceptance of music has reached an all time high, allowing for new experimentation and new genres.
